Advantages of Time-frequency Analysis vs Waveform-based Analysis:

  • Time-frequency analysis offers better insight into the tempo, beat, texture, and timbre of a song/sound file.
  • As seen in the examples above, many spectrograms contain large vertical lines that indicate either a high burst of noise or a conglomeration of noise from several instruments. This can be helpful in determining how many beats are in a bar.
  • Similarly, instrumentation can be a lot easier to identifying using a spectrogram. Many instruments operate at a high frequency, many with mid frequencies, and some at lower frquencies.
  • As seen in the first example, Frame for the Blues, the trumpet melodies can be seen higher up, many other brass and saxiohone lines seen in the middle, and many trombones/bass lines can be found at the bottom.
